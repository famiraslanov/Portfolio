Summary

The Lancaster County (Pa.) Employees Retirement Fund is a defined benefit pension that serves public employees in Lancaster, Pennsylvania, US.

Investment intentions
February 2022
  • The system approved an annual $2.5m investment for private equity funds to achieve its recently established 5% target. Dollar value may change to remain consistent with the plan's investment policy statement.
  • Monies invested in the Cohen & Steers Global Infrastructure Fund sit in the investment queue for the IFM Global Infrastructure Fund and the JP Morgan Infrastructure Investments Fund.
