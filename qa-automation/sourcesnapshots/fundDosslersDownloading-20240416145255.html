The Essentials

Engaging with our community, mobilizing data and intel from numerous sources heres what you need to know about Ares Specialty Healthcare Fund.

Ares Specialty Healthcare Fund will target established commercial-stage specialty healthcare companies, providing flexible growth financing. The strategy will primarily target investments in floating-rate, first lien, senior secured loans. However, it has the flexibility to invest across the capital structure with both sponsored and non-sponsored companies.

Offered in a levered and unlevered format, the fund will consist of around 30-45 investments with a 2% to 3% position size. The fund will invest primarily around 80% of its capital in North America, with the remainder going to Europe.

Ares is looking to build a portfolio based around five key subsectors: pharmaceuticals and biotech, diagnostics, medical devices, specialty services, and healthcare tech. The fund is intended to focus on therapies for rare diseases and life science tools while avoiding general services like dental care.

The fund is seeking a return of 12%+ net IRR for its unlevered sleeve and is looking to raise $1.5bn.
