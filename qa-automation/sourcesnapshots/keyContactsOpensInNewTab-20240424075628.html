Bio

Timothy O'Connell, CFA is the Director of Liquid Strategies at Fire & Police Pension Association of Colorado. Previously, he served as the Head of Equities at NEPC, LLC, overseeing public market equity, equity hedge fund, and opportunistic equity strategies globally. Prior to that, he held various research consultant and analyst positions at NEPC, LLC, focusing on public markets, equities, and hedge funds. Timothy holds a BA in Political Science from the University of California, Berkeley and attended Belmont Hill School.
